Opening

All Officers in their places.

DC - (from door - knocks) Brethren, please rise to receive your WM.

(Brethren rise. DC proceeds in front of WM who takes his place in E. If all is in order DC bows to WM. DC takes his place.

WM gives 1K, and is answered by the S and JWs.)

Brethren, assist me to open the lodge. (All rise)

(to JW, calling him by name) Bro. A, what is the first and constant care of every Mason?

(no Sn) To see that the Lodge is properly T...d.

(to JW) Direct that duty to be done.

(to IG by name), Bro B, see that the Lodge is properly tiled.

(Opens the door of the lodge; and after seeing that the T is at his post, he closes the door, turns round, and reports to the JW by name without Sn) Bro. A, the Lodge is properly T...d.

(Gives IK, bows to the WM) W Bro. D, the Lodge is properly T...d.

(to SW, by name) Bro. C, what is the next care?

To see that none but Masons are present.

Do you vouch that all present are Masons?

With your assistance, W Bro. E, I do.

(Gives IK, followed by SW and JW) To order, Brethren, as Masons.

(The Brethren stand to order as EA FMs).

Bro. JW, how many principal officers are there in the Lodge?

Three, the WM, and the S and JWs.

Bro. SW, how many assistant officers are there?

Likewise three, besides the T or OG, namely the S and JDs and the IG.

(to JW) The situation of the T or OG?

Without the entrance of the Lodge.

(to JW) What is his duty?

Being armed with a Dn. Sd, to keep off all intruders and cowans to Masonry, and to see that the Candidates are properly prepared.

(to SW) The situation of the IG?

Within the entrance to the Lodge.

(to IG) Bro. IG, your duty?

To answer all reports, to admit Masons on proof, to receive the Candidates in due form, and to be of assistance to the JW.

(to JW) Where is the situation of the JD?

At the right of and near to the SW.

(to JD) Bro. JD, your duty?

To carry all messages and commands of the WM from the SW to the JW that the same may be punctually obeyed.

(to SW) The situation of the SD?

At the right of and near to the WM.

(to SD) Bro. SD, your duty?

To bear all messages and commands of the WM to the SW and await the return of the JD.

(to JW) Your constant place in the Lodge?

In the S.

(to JW) Why are you so placed?

To mark the Sun at its Meridian, to call the Brethren from labour to refreshment, and from refreshment to labour, that profit and pleasure may be the result.

(to SW) Bro. SW, your constant place in the Lodge?

In the W.

(to SW) Why are you so placed?

To mark the Setting Sun, to close the Lodge by command of the WM, after having seen that every Brother has had his just due.

(to IPM) W and W IPM, The Master's place?

In the E.

(to IPM) Why is he so placed?

As the Sun rises in the E to open and enliven the day, so the WM is placed in the E to open the Lodge, and employ and instruct the Brethren in the principles of Freemasonry.

Brethren stand with the sign of F.

or CHAPLAIN - The Lodge being duly formed, before it is declared open, let us invoke a blessing from the GAOTU on all our undertakings. May our labours thus begun in order, be conducted in peace, and closed in harmony.

So mote it be.

Brethren, in the name of the GAOTU I declare this Lodge open for the purposes of Freemasonry in the First Degree.

(WM gives proper Ks with LH and all drop the Sn. Ks are repeated by the SW, who raises his Cn, by the JW, who lowers his Cn, and by the IG and T.

The IPM opens the V of the SL and places the Sq and C thereon with Pts covered.

The SD and JD attend to TB)

Bro. O, the opening ode.

(WM sits, signalling the brethren to sit.

Minutes are then read and confirmed with any alterations agreed upon.

WM welcomes visitors. Announcement of deaths, almoner's report on sick brethren are then usually taken.

WM proceeds with business on summons)
